# User Research

**ประเภท**: Model-invoked (ไม่มี argument-hint)
**Trigger**: เรียกใช้โดยอัตโนมัติเมื่อต้องการ research plan, interview guide, usability test, survey design, research questions

## ภาพรวม

วางแผน ดำเนินการ และสังเคราะห์ user research studies

### Research Methods
| Method | สำหรับ | Sample Size | ระยะเวลา |
|--------|--------|-------------|----------|
| User interviews | Understanding ต้องการและแรงจูงใจ | 5-8 | 2-4 weeks |
| Usability testing | Evaluating specific design/flow | 5-8 | 1-2 weeks |
| Surveys | Quantifying attitudes/preferences | 100+ | 1-2 weeks |
| Card sorting | Information architecture | 15-30 | 1 week |
| Diary studies | Understanding behavior over time | 10-15 | 2-8 weeks |
| A/B testing | Comparing specific choices | Statistical | 1-4 weeks |

### Interview Guide Structure
1. **Warm-up** (5 min): Build rapport, explain session
2. **Context** (10 min): Current workflow
3. **Deep dive** (20 min): Topic-specific exploration
4. **Reaction** (10 min): Concepts/prototypes
5. **Wrap-up** (5 min): Gaps? Thank them.

### Analysis Frameworks
- Affinity mapping: Group observations → themes
- Impact/effort matrix: Prioritize findings
- Journey mapping: Visualize experience over time
- Jobs to be done: What are users "hiring" your product for?

## Deliverables

- Research plan (objectives, methods, timeline, participants)
- Interview guide (questions, probes, activities)
- Synthesis report (themes, insights, recommendations)
- Highlight reel (key quotes and observations)

## เชื่อมโยงกับ

- **research-synthesis**: Analyze and distill findings
