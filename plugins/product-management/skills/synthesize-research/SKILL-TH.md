# Synthesize Research — สังเคราะห์ User Research

**ประเภท**: User-invoked (slash command)

**Trigger**: `/synthesize-research <research topic or question>`

## ที่เก็บข้อมูล

สังเคราะห์ user research จาก interviews, surveys และ feedback เป็น insights ที่มีโครงสร้าง

## คุณสมบัติหลัก

- **Thematic Analysis** — Familiarization, coding, theme development, review, refinement
- **Affinity Mapping** — Cluster observations, label clusters, identify themes
- **Triangulation** — Combine multiple data sources and methods
- **Interview Analysis** — Extract observations, behaviors, quotes, signals of intensity
- **Survey Interpretation** — Quantitative analysis, open-ended response analysis
- **Persona Development** — Evidence-based personas from behavioral patterns
- **Opportunity Sizing** — Estimate addressable users, frequency, severity, impact

## Output Format

Structured synthesis with overview, findings (5-8 major), segments/personas, opportunities, recommendations, open questions

## ใช้เมื่อ

- Have pile of interview notes, survey responses, support tickets
- Need to extract themes and rank findings by frequency and impact
- Want to turn raw feedback into roadmap recommendations

## เชื่อมโยงกับ

- Knowledge base (search research documents, interview notes)
- User feedback tools (Intercom — pull support tickets, feature requests)
- Product analytics (pull usage data, behavioral patterns)
- Meeting transcription (pull interview recordings, summaries)
