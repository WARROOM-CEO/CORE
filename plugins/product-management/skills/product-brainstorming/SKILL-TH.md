# Product Brainstorming — Thinking Partner สำหรับ PM

**ประเภท**: Model-invoked (auto-triggered, no slash command)

**Trigger**: Appears when Claude detects product thinking conversations; can also be invoked through `/brainstorm`

## ที่เก็บข้อมูล

คู่คิด PM ที่ challenge assumptions, ถามคำถามยากๆ และผลักดัน ideas ให้ไปไกลขึ้น

## คุณสมบัติหลัก

- **Problem Exploration** — Understand problem spaces deeply before jumping to solutions
- **Solution Ideation** — Generate multiple distinct approaches (not just one)
- **Assumption Testing** — Stress-test ideas, find weak points, test riskiest assumptions
- **Strategy Exploration** — Explore strategic moves, bets, second-order effects
- **Brainstorming Frameworks** — How Might We, JTBD, First Principles, Opportunity Solution Trees, SCAMPER, OODA Loop, Reverse Brainstorming
- **Session Structure** — Frame → Diverge → Provoke → Converge → Capture

## Output Format

Conversational sparring, not a deliverable. Key ideas with supporting rationale, strongest directions, riskiest assumptions, suggested next steps.

## ใช้เมื่อ

- Explore opportunity areas ใหม่ๆ
- Generate solutions to product problems
- Stress-test assumptions และ ideas
- Need sparring partner ก่อนจะตัดสินใจทิศทาง

## เชื่อมโยงกับ

- Knowledge base (prior research, specs, decisions)
- Product analytics (ground ideas in usage data)
- Project tracker (check what has been tried before)
- Chat (review team discussions for context)
