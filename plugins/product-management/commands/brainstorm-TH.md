# Brainstorm — Spark Product Thinking

**ประเภท**: User-invoked (slash command)

**Trigger**: `/brainstorm <topic, problem, or idea to explore>`

## ที่เก็บข้อมูล

Brainstorm ใน product topic กับ thinking partner ที่ opinionated — conversation, ไม่ใช่ deliverable

## คุณสมบัติหลัก

- **Problem Exploration Mode** — Understand problem spaces deeply, surface root causes
- **Solution Ideation Mode** — Generate 5-7 distinct approaches, vary along meaningful dimensions
- **Assumption Testing Mode** — Stress-test ideas, find weak points, test riskiest assumptions
- **Strategy Exploration Mode** — Explore strategic moves, bets, second-order effects
- **Brainstorming Frameworks** — HMW, JTBD, First Principles, Opportunity Solution Trees, SCAMPER, OODA Loop, Reverse Brainstorming
- **Session Structure** — Frame → Diverge → Provoke → Converge → Capture

## Output Format

Conversational engagement. Close with: key ideas (2-5), strongest direction, riskiest assumption, suggested next step, parked ideas.

## ใช้เมื่อ

- Explore opportunity areas ใหม่ๆ
- Test assumptions before converging
- Apply PM frameworks (HMW, JTBD, First Principles)
- Need sparring partner ก่อนจะตัดสินใจ

## เชื่อมโยงกับ

- Knowledge base (pull prior research, specs, decisions)
- Product analytics (ground ideas in usage data)
- Project tracker (check what has been tried)
- Chat (review team discussions)
